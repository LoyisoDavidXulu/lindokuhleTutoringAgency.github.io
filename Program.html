Our Programs

Program Details

Our tutoring programs are designed to meet the unique needs of each student. We offer three different programs: Basic, Standard, and Premium.

  • Basic: Our Basic program is designed for students who need extra help in one or two subjects.
  • Standard: Our Standard program includes video resources for each subject, and is ideal for students who want to deepen their understanding of the material.
  • Premium: Our Premium program includes one-on-one tutoring sessions, and is perfect for students who need individualized attention and support.
